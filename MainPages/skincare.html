Formulations to support the skin's needs

For 'normal skin

Neither oily, nor dry—better described as ‘balanced’.

For dry skin

Can be fragile, flaky, and dull in appearance due to a lack of sebum.

For oily skin

Often shiny in appearance, with large pores due to an over-supply of sebum.

For combination skin

Both oily and dry in nature, with a greater propensity to reactivity.

For mature skin

Naturally depleted, with diminishing oil production and moisture retention.

For sensative skin

Delicate and prone to aggravation and reactivity; can affect all skin types.
